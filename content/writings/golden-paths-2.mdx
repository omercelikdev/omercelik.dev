> Example post, part two of the series.

If the golden path is the road, the **manifest** is the map everyone agrees on.
A single `manifest.yaml` (plus OpenAPI / AsyncAPI) becomes the source of truth,
and tooling checks the repo against it.

## Why it matters for AI

When the spec is explicit and machine-readable, AI can generate against it and a
deterministic linter can verify the result — no LLM in the validation loop:

```yaml
# .goldpath/manifest.yaml (illustrative)
service: orders
contracts:
  - openapi: ./api/orders.yaml
invariants:
  - every endpoint has an idempotency key
```

Humans approve, guardrails verify, drift gets caught early. That's the whole idea.
